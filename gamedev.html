Gods and Shadows

Gods and Shadows is a JRPG mixing narrative elements and turn based combat with semi-roguelike progression.

I worked on this game as a Writer and Narrative designer with the GIMM Works development team. This was made in partnership with the Make-A-Wish foundation.

Play Gods and Shadows here

Bounties and Brew

Bounties and Brew is a VR Game where you are a space alchemist who has a dual life as a bounty hunter to make ends meet and pay off their debts.

This game is my first solo VR project, created in Unity for the Meta Quest 3.


Sidekick Search

Sidekick search is a 360 degree video VR experience themed after a noir detective style story.

This project is one of my first forays into Virtual Reality in Unity. This experience was made in part by Drew Peppley, Chandlor Stauff, Nick Clohessy, and myself!
